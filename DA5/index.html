Starship Battlefield

  • Originality: It's a 2D ship battle simulator
  • Technical Merit: The way I was able to figure out how to angle the sprite properly as well as handle collision detection for the bullets.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What did you learn from this prototype? -I learned a lot about collisions and the best way to handle them. It was difficult at first as I tried many different methods to handle a group of objects colliding with a single sprite, but I eventually figured it out. What was the easiest or hardest part of making it? -The easiest part was figure out how to handle the velocity. The hardest part was either angling the sprites properly or handling the collision of the bullets with the player objects.
